Operations

All reservation payments are deposited into an audited trust account. We manage the rental process and maintenance of a wide variety of privately owned properties. We have a fully staffed operation in Front Desk, Accounting, Housekeeping, Laundry, Maintenance and Landscaping. We strive to provide our guests with excellent service and value by offering a wide selection of properties for guests to rent. Our Guest Service Scores earned us a SuperHost status on Airbnb in 1997. We have had a consistent level of 5 Star Ratings in our Housekeeping department. We have a unique standard in housekeeping, that allows our property owners to buy colorful quality linen and towels which provides our guests with an upscale resort experience. .
